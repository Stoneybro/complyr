## Auditor Portal

![Auditor Portal](/complyr-audit-portal.png)

The Auditor Portal is a dedicated interface that allows external auditors and regulators to verify a company's audit records without accessing the company's main dashboard.

### The Simple Flow

1. **Authorization:** A company explicitly authorizes their external auditor's wallet address in the Complyr dashboard.
2. **Sharing Access:** The company generates a secure **Auditor Access Link** (`/auditors/[proxyAddress]`) and shares it with the auditor.
3. **Auditor Login:** The auditor navigates to the portal and connects their authorized wallet (MetaMask or any injected provider).
4. **Visibility:** The auditor clicks **Decrypt Audit Data**, and successfully views all records and downloads audit reports without having actual login access to the company's bank account.

### Under the Hood (Zama FHE)

This system is powered by encrypted computation, not ordinary role-based access to plaintext records.

**Private reviewer limits:** The auditor creates a private limit in the browser. That limit is encrypted with Zama tooling before submission to `AuditRegistry`.

**Encrypted evaluation:** When new records are appended, the contract checks those encrypted records against the encrypted reviewer test. The contract can compute whether a payment exceeds the auditor's limit without seeing the plaintext value.

**User-authorized decryption:** When the auditor chooses to decrypt findings or records, the browser requests a Zama decryption authorization flow and only decrypts the handles the auditor is allowed to access.

**What auditors can see.** Auditors only have access to the encrypted outputs they are permitted to decrypt. When granted access, they can view:
- Plaintext payment metadata (transaction hash, date, recipients, amounts)
- Decrypted expense category per recipient
- Decrypted regulatory jurisdiction per recipient
- Reviewer findings and encrypted report outputs based on access level

Auditors cannot modify records, authorise additional parties, or access company data beyond what they explicitly have the key for.

### Auditor portal URL format

```text
https://usecomplyr.vercel.app/auditors/{proxyAccountAddress}
```

Where `proxyAccountAddress` is the company's smart wallet address.
